Recipe of the Month

Gourmet Mushroom Risotto

45 mins 4 servings

Ingredients

  • Arborio Rice - 1.5 cups
  • Mushrooms - 500g
  • Vegetable Broth - 4 cups
  • White Wine - 1/2 cup
  • Parmesan Cheese - 1 cup
  • Shallots - 2 medium

Instructions

  1. Heat broth in saucepan over medium heat
  2. Sauté mushrooms and shallots in olive oil
  3. Add rice and toast for 2 minutes
  4. Deglaze with white wine
  5. Add broth gradually while stirring
  6. Stir in parmesan before serving
